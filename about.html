About Me

Arav Watwani

Aside from being a full-time student at Westview High School, I am a determined photographer, developer, and musician. I have been photographing for 6 years, often finding myself in precarious scenarios chasing the thrill of the perfect shot and experiencing new places. To me, photography as an extension of the human experience; a visual language that is absorbed and read. My love for photography has served as the perfect way to elicit responses in viewers and let them live through my eyes at any particular moment.
